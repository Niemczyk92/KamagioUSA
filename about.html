Our Founding Story

How Magdi and I created Kamagio from a daily need.

Kamagio was born amidst busy days when my wife Magdi and I were constantly traveling, working, and exercising. Our drinks always seemed to go lukewarm. We searched for a stylish, practical, and versatile bottle that could keep up with our fast-paced lives. When we couldn't find one that met our standards, we decided to create it ourselves.

This everyday frustration sparked the idea for Kamagio. Our goal was not just to keep beverages at the perfect temperature, but also to offer a product that felt authentic, practical, and filled with a love for simple, yet effective solutions. Every tumbler and bottle you find here is a result of our personal touch and our desire to solve a problem we knew all too well.
