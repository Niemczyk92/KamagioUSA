Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how KAMAGIO ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you visit, use our services, or make a purchase from kamagio.com (the "Site").

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information about you from various sources, including directly from you, through automated technologies, and from third-party sources. The types of information we collect include:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping address.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address (processed securely by third-party payment processors).
  • Order Details: Products purchased, order history.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages viewed, time spent on site, referring URLs.
  • Device Information: Device type, unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide products and services: Fulfilling orders, processing payments, shipping, customer support.
  • For marketing and advertising: Sending promotional emails (with your consent), personalizing ads.
  • For security and fraud prevention: Protecting against fraudulent transactions, unauthorized access.
  • For analytics and improvement: Understanding website usage, improving our products and services.
  • For legal compliance: Meeting legal obligations, resolving disputes.

3. Sharing Your Personal Information

We may share your personal information with third parties for the purposes outlined above, including:

  • Service Providers: Companies that assist us with payment processing, shipping, marketing, web hosting.
  • Business Partners: For joint marketing efforts or specific product offerings.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect our rights.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Access: Requesting access to your personal information.
  • Correction: Requesting correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Requesting deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt-out: Opting out of marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your Browse behavior. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is entirely secure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The revised policy will be posted on this page with the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at: privacy@kamagio.com.
