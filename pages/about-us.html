    About Us

    At KAMAGIO, we believe that hydration should feel as good as it looks.
    Our journey started with a simple problem — during long days of travel, work, and workouts, our drinks were never the right temperature. Instead of settling, we decided to create something better — bottles and tumblers that combine style, quality, and practicality.

    Founded by Kamil and Magda, KAMAGIO is built on a passion for design and a love for everyday simplicity. Each product reflects our belief that small details matter — from premium stainless steel and vacuum insulation to elegant colors and timeless design.

    The name KAMAGIO comes from us — Kamil and Magdi. It represents more than a brand; it’s a story of partnership, creativity, and shared inspiration. Every bottle and tumbler we make carries a touch of who we are — durable, functional, and made to last.

    We create limited editions inspired by real moments from our lives — peaceful mornings, ocean sunsets, mountain adventures. Our goal is to help you enjoy every sip and remember that beauty often lies in simplicity.

    But KAMAGIO isn’t just about products — it’s about community. Our customers are people who value authenticity, individuality, and thoughtful design. Owning a KAMAGIO tumbler means joining a growing circle of people who appreciate quality and want to make everyday hydration something special.

    Join us on this journey.
    Drink with purpose. Live with style. Stay authentic.
